Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping, exchanges, and AI Stylist.

How do I place an order?

Add products of your desired size to the cart. Click "Confirm & Send Invoice on WhatsApp" to coordinate directly with the store owner, or choose "Pay Securely" to simulate an instant payment and order verification.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery is absolutely FREE for all orders above ₹1000! For orders below ₹1000, a small shipping fee of ₹50 is applied.

Can I try clothes virtually before buying?

Yes! Go to the "AI Stylist" tab on the storefront. You can choose a model avatar or take a live picture using your device webcam, and drag and resize any clothing catalog item over your picture to see the perfect fit!

How do I exchange an item?

Exchanges are allowed within a 7-day window once the order status is updated to "Delivered". Go to your "Order History" tab, click the "Exchange Item" button on your delivered order card, select the item and the sizing/color reason, and submit it.

How do loyalty points work?

You earn 1 point for every ₹10 spent on the final checkout subtotal. When your points balance is 200 points or more, you can check the "Loyalty Points Discount" box during checkout to redeem them for direct discounts at a rate of 1 point = ₹0.50.

Return & Exchange Policy

Smart Collection, Jalalpur strives for 100% customer satisfaction.

7-Day Sizing & Color Exchange

We offer a hassle-free **7-day exchange window** for size adjustments, defective garments, or color switches once an order is marked as **Delivered**.

Terms & Conditions:

  • All garments submitted for exchange must be **unworn, unwashed, and undamaged** with original brand tags attached.
  • Accompany the return package with a printed copy of the invoice (which can be downloaded as a PDF from your Order History tab).
  • For self-pickup orders, please bring the garment directly to our store in Jalalpur Chowk for an instant exchange.
  • For delivery orders, once you submit the online exchange request, our delivery partner will coordinate the return pick-up and deliver the new sizing within 48-72 hours upon administrator approval.

Important Notice:

Decisions made by the Store Administrator regarding return approvals or rejections are tracked and logged. The administrator is required to provide clear feedback reasons on any rejected exchange requests, which will be visible inside your Customer Profile panel.

Privacy & Data Security Policy

Your trust and privacy are our top priorities at Smart Collection.

Data Protection Standards

This policy details how Smart Collection, Jalalpur collects, stores, and protects customer data for checkout and account rewards:

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic customer information when you register via OTP login, Google Sign-in, or complete a checkout order: Mobile phone number, profile name, delivery address line, wishlist bookmarks, and purchase order logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your contact number and shipping address are strictly used to fulfill your purchase deliveries and calculate loyalty cashback points. We do not use third-party advertisements or sell customer databases under any circumstances.

3. Storage and Safety

All profiles, orders, and review comments are stored securely in a MongoDB Atlas database cluster with sparse-index encryption, ensuring your personal contact details remain private and safe.
